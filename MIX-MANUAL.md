# 🧱 GLYXON PRIMALS: Concrete Mix & Curing Manual
> **High-Performance Infrastructure for Molecular Sovereignty.**

This document describes the Ultra-High Performance Concrete (UHPC) recipe required to cast the GLYXON PRIMALS chassis. The goal is a dense, thermally stable, and vibration-resistant body.

## 🧪 The "Primal" Recipe (By Weight)
To ensure the bunker withstands the thermal stress of 35+ PCR cycles (95°C peaks), use the following ratios:

| Component | Ratio | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Portland Cement (Type I/II)** | 1.0 | The binder matrix. |
| **Fine Silica Sand (40-60 Mesh)** | 1.5 | Structural stability & reduced shrinkage. |
| **Silica Fume (Optional)** | 0.1 | Particle packing for a glass-like finish. |
| **Distilled Water** | 0.3 - 0.35 | Low W/C ratio for maximum strength. |
| **Polycarboxylate Superplasticizer** | 1.5% | Ensures flowability into silicone molds. |
| **Alkali-Resistant (AR) Glass Fibers** | 0.05 | Micro-crack prevention during thermal ramping. |

## 🛠 Casting Protocol
1. **Dry Mix:** Blend cement, sand, and fibers until color is uniform.
2. **Activation:** Dissolve the superplasticizer in the distilled water.
3. **Integration:** Pour liquid into dry mix slowly. Do **NOT** add extra water. The mix will turn liquid as the plasticizer activates.
4. **Vibration:** Pour into the silicone mold from a height of 30cm to break air bubbles. Use a vibration table or orbital sander for 5 minutes.
5. **Insert Contacts:** Place the brass galvanic contacts (Wireless Lid System) while the concrete is still "green" (approx. 2 hours after pouring).

## 💧 Curing for Precision
* **Initial Set:** Keep covered in plastic for 24 hours.
* **Hydration Bath:** Submerge the de-molded piece in a 40°C water bath for 3 days to maximize structural integrity.
* **Post-Curing:** Air dry for 48 hours before applying graffiti or internal electronics.
