🏗️ Glyxon Manufacturing Philosophy
Material Sovereignty: All support feet and internal rigs must be printed using recycled filament(rPET/rPLA). This aligns the project with a circular economy and frugal science principles.
Thermal Resilience: For internal parts near the 36 IR LED matrix, rPETG or high-temp recycled polymers are mandatory to prevent structural warping during cycles.
Silhouette Precision: The 0.22 cm aluminum mirrors must be precision-cut to fit the 3D printed slots before the concrete pouring process to ensure perfect optical alignment.
