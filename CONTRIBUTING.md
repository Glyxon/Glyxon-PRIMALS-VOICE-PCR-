# 📢 Join the Resistance: Contributing to GLYXON PRIMALS
> **Scientific sovereignty is not built; it is claimed. Join us in evolving Personal Justice (PJ).**

Welcome! GLYXON PRIMALS is a community-driven project. Whether you are a molecular biologist, a street artist, a firmware engineer, or a concrete enthusiast, your contribution is vital to the democratization of science.

## 🤖 Evolving PJ (Personal Justice)
**PJ** is the conversational core of the PRIMALS bunker. We are looking for contributors to help with:
* **Neural Voice Integration:** Improving local-first STT (Speech-to-Text) for complex biological nomenclature.
* **Protocol Intelligence:** Developing the logic for PJ to verify PCR checklists and suggest real-time corrections.
* **Matter Clusters:** Mapping laboratory sensors into the Home Assistant ecosystem.

## 🧱 Hardware & Aesthetics
* **The Bunker:** Iterating on the UHPC concrete mix and silicone mold designs for better thermal stability and structural durability.
* **Street Art Integration:** We invite artists to submit stencils and patterns for the PRIMALS chassis, merging brutalist form with scientific function.
* **Galvanic Lid:** Refining the wireless 1/4 turn contact system for maximum electrical reliability.

## 🧬 Open Biology & General Reagents
* **Polymerase Optimization:** Testing heat-stable, high-fidelity enzymes within the PRIMALS photonic environment.
* **Protocol Standardization:** Documenting open-source methods for protein purification and DNA amplification for decentralized laboratories.

## 🛠 How to Contribute
1. **Fork the Repo:** Create your own branch for experimental features.
2. **Open a Discussion:** Use the "Discussions" tab for architectural proposals or new biomaterial ideas.
3. **Submit a Pull Request (PR):** Ensure your commit messages follow our brutalist clarity (e.g., `feCONTRIBUTING.mdat: improve PJ's voice for DNA melting curves`).
